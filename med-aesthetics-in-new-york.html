Frequently Asked Questions About Med Aesthetics

How much do your med aesthetic treatments typically cost in New York City?
At ARK Esthetics, pricing for med aesthetic treatments varies depending on the service and complexity. For example, neuromodulator injections like Botox start at $350 per area, while dermal fillers begin at $700 per syringe. We offer transparent, upfront pricing during your consultation, and we’re happy to provide itemized quotes tailored to your treatment plan. Please note that prices in NYC may be slightly higher compared to other areas due to overhead costs.
How long does it take to see results from your treatments, and how long do they last?
Most clients begin to notice results from injectable treatments like Botox or fillers within 3-7 days, with full effects visible in about two weeks. The longevity of results varies: neuromodulators typically last 3-4 months, while dermal fillers can last anywhere from 6 months to over a year, depending on the product and treatment area. We’ll discuss a personalized timeline during your initial visit at our New York office.
Are your providers licensed and trained for both orthodontic and aesthetic procedures?
Absolutely. ARK Esthetics is led by board-certified orthodontists who have undergone additional accredited training and certification in medical aesthetics. Our providers are licensed in New York State for all procedures we offer, and we prioritize ongoing education to stay current with the latest standards in both orthodontics and aesthetics.
