Frequently Asked Questions About Orthodontics

How much do braces or Invisalign treatments typically cost at ARK Esthetics in New York City?
At ARK Esthetics, the cost for traditional braces generally ranges from $4,000 to $7,500, while Invisalign treatments usually start around $5,500 and can go up depending on the complexity of your case. Our New York City location accepts most major dental insurance plans, and we offer flexible payment plans to help make orthodontic care more accessible. We’re happy to provide a personalized estimate during your complimentary consultation.
How long does the average orthodontic treatment take at your New York office?
The length of orthodontic treatment at ARK Esthetics varies depending on individual needs, but most patients in New York complete their treatment in 12 to 24 months. Minor corrections with clear aligners can sometimes be completed in as little as 6 months, while more complex cases with braces may take up to 30 months. During your initial visit, our orthodontist will provide a detailed timeline based on your specific goals.
Is ARK Esthetics licensed and are your orthodontists board-certified in New York?
Yes, ARK Esthetics is fully licensed to provide orthodontic services in New York State. Our lead orthodontist is board-certified by the American Board of Orthodontics and is a member of both the American Association of Orthodontists and the New York State Dental Association. We prioritize ongoing education and the highest clinical standards for all our providers.
What is the process for starting orthodontic treatment at ARK Esthetics?
When you visit our Manhattan office, your first appointment will include a comprehensive consultation, digital X-rays, and a 3D scan of your teeth. Our orthodontist will discuss your goals, explain your treatment options (braces, clear aligners, etc.), and answer any questions. We’ll provide a detailed treatment plan and cost estimate. If you’re ready, we can take impressions and get you started right away. We tailor every step to fit your schedule as a busy New Yorker.
Do you offer any guarantees or follow-up care after orthodontic treatment?
At ARK Esthetics, we stand by the quality of our work. After your braces or aligner treatment, we provide a retention phase with custom retainers and regular follow-up visits to ensure your results last. If you experience any shifting within the first year post-treatment, we offer complimentary minor adjustments. Our team is always available for questions or emergencies, and we’re committed to helping you maintain your new smile for years to come.
