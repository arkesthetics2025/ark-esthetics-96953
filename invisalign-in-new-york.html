How long does Invisalign treatment take at your New York office?
The length of Invisalign treatment at ARK Esthetics depends on your orthodontic needs, but most patients in New York complete treatment in 12 to 18 months. Is ARK Esthetics licensed to provide Invisalign treatment in New York State?
Yes, ARK Esthetics is fully licensed in New York State, and our orthodontists are certified Invisalign providers. Our doctors stay up-to-date with the latest Invisalign training and technology, so you can be confident you’re receiving expert care that meets all state and local standards.
What is the process for starting Invisalign at ARK Esthetics?
Your Invisalign journey at ARK Esthetics starts with a complimentary consultation where we use 3D digital scanning technology—meaning no messy impressions—to assess your teeth and bite. We’ll schedule regular follow-ups at our NYC office to monitor your progress and provide support throughout your treatment.
